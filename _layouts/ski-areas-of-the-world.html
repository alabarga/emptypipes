Usage

  • Clicking on one of the links to the right will center the map on that ski area.
  • The list on the right is long. Scroll using the scroll wheel on your mouse or with a trackpad gesture
  • Clicking the 'layers' icon in the upper right hand corner lets you change the map (e.g. to topo)
  • Annotated ski areas are colored green
  • Unannotated ski areas are colored red
  • The currently selected ski area is colored yellow irregardless of whether it is selected or not

Annotation

If you know the name of a ski area that hasn't been annotated in the list to the right of the map, please post a comment below and I'll try and add it lickety split.
